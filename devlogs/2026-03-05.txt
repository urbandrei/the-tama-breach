Title: DEEP PEN overhaul — device OS, death screens, and more
TL;DR: Massive update — new main menu, home screen OS on the device, snake minigame, jumpscare death sequence, proximity effects, and tons of bug fixes and gameplay tweaks.
---
Bug fixes:
- Water bowl is now pickable even when pipe repair task is in the same room
- Glass repair works from anywhere on the glass wall, not just a tiny trigger point
- Shelves/props have larger invisible hitboxes so interactions don't miss between gaps
- Lure only appears interactable when a specimen has actually escaped
- Minimap escort paths no longer clip through walls
- If a specimen hatches from the cart while still in the elevator, it now properly spawns next to the cart and walks out — cart stays visible until the creature leaves, then elevator doors close

Gameplay changes:
- Escaped specimens get faster and more aggressive the longer they're out
- No tasks spawn in the last minute — time to clock out
- Lure glows during containment breach so you can find it
- Charging station shows a progress bar and minimap path when device is dead
- Specimens can break items in their cage during tantrums — you'll need to re-fetch them
- Glass repair works by clicking anywhere on the containment glass
- Visible 3D care buttons on containment room walls (orange=FEED, blue=WATER, purple=PLAY)
- Clock-out sequence at 5AM — interact with terminal, then get to the elevator before 6AM or it's game over
- Supply restock task appears periodically in the storage room (night 2+)
- Food processing room now has its own sorting task

Device overhaul:
- Device has a proper home screen with app icons instead of tabs
- Apps open in windows with [X] close button
- Snake minigame playable on the device (pure time-killer)
- Task app now shows all task TYPES with ACTIVE/PENDING/IDLE status
- Care items are drag-and-drop — drag food/water/toys onto the specimen

New features:
- Main menu on the device at game start — START, SETTINGS, CREDITS
- Renamed to DEEP PEN throughout

Polish:
- 5-phase jumpscare death screen: white flash → specimen close-up → TV static → black → retry
- Each specimen has unique visual effects when nearby: Void darkens vision, Glitch tears the screen, Feral pulses red edges
- Camera tilts slightly when strafing
- Strafe tilt for extra immersion
