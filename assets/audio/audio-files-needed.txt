THE TAMA BREACH — Audio Files Manifest
=======================================

All audio files needed for the game, organized by category.
Format: OGG or MP3 recommended. Keep files small for web loading.


=== MUSIC ===

music/ambient_calm.ogg
  Calm ambient loop for normal gameplay. Low drone, subtle synth pads.
  Plays during PLAYING state when no tamas are agitated or escaped.

music/ambient_tense.ogg
  Tense ambient loop. Darker tone, uneasy undercurrent, subtle heartbeat.
  Crossfades from calm when a tama becomes agitated or containment is cracking.

music/chase_theme.ogg
  Intense chase music. Fast tempo, aggressive synth, driving percussion.
  Crossfades in when an escaped tama enters CHASE state toward player.

music/night_intro.ogg
  Short sting (3-5s) for night intro screen. Ominous, sets the mood.
  Plays once when "NIGHT X" overlay appears.

music/night_complete.ogg
  Short success sting (3-5s). Relief, minor triumph.
  Plays once when "NIGHT COMPLETE" overlay appears.

music/victory.ogg
  Longer victory theme (10-15s). Triumph, relief after final night.
  Plays on "SHIFT COMPLETE" screen after Night 5.
